            <h1 style="margin-left: 0cm; text-indent: 0cm;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 14.0pt;">3. Change Control</span></h1>
            <p style="text-align: justify;line-height: 16.0pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 14.0pt;">Any change to the Deliverables that a party wishes to make, may only be made by following the change control procedure set out in this section 3. A party is not obliged to accept any change to the Deliverables that has not been approved following the change control procedure set out in this section 3.</span></p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;line-height: 16.0pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 14.0pt;">Where during the term of the Agreement a party (Initiator) desires a change to the Deliverables (each a Change), the Initiator will notify the other party (Other Party) in writing giving reasonable particulars of the change and any impact that the Initiator anticipates on the overall delivery of the Services (Change Notice).</span></p>
